About us

About: Book Reward started as a timid project, set up by two book nerds who wanted to work with a product they are passionate about- books. As an independent book-seller we strive to give our customers not only the product they are looking for, but also make our platform easy to navigate, hence our simple but practical design. We offer both new and old books, aiming to cater for as wide a variety of readers as possible. Our Mission: To provide books to as many reading enthusiasts as we can. Book Reward is run independently by its two co founders, Andra and Roxana. They both hope to become a relevant part of the book market in the years to come.
